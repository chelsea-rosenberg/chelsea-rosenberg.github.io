In the past, when I've asked mentors and coworkers how they found themselves as a Recruiter, the answer tends to boil down to a simple "I fell into it".

Recruiting isn't one of those fields you dream about when you're little. Have you ever heard a 5 year old say that they want to be a Recruiter when they grow up? Nope. You also don't graduate with a degree in Recruiting, it seems to be one of those careers that you try and either love it or hate it.

I'll give you one guess as to which side of the fence I fell on... did you guess it? Yup! I fell flat on my face in love with recruiting and I can hardly wait to help you find a job you love just as much.

Helping someone find a job that lets them be them, allows them flexibility to grow, that's the best. That is why I'm in this business.

I will find you a job you love or a creative who will love to support your team, you can count on it
